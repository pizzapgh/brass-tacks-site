Wash & Wax

Chemical Guys EcoSmart Concentrate cleans, shines, & protects using a unique eco-based natural carnauba to emulsify and lift dirt away from the surface. Dresser

V.R.P. Super Shine Dressing is a dry-to-the-touch water-based dressing that creates an invisible shield repelling water, dirt & dust. It also protects surfaces from damaging UV rays, resists drying, fading and cracking surfaces while at the same time enhancing shine & overall vehicle

Gloss
Enhancer

Designed to deliver a deep, wet shine that lasts while also reducing the static charge that attracts dust and removes fingerprints. Zero streaks with no film or wax, forming an ultra-thin protective coat that bonds to waxes and synthetic sealants leaving the surface slick and shining.
